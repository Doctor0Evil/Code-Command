# 100 Research Questions, Definition Requests, Detail Queries, and Objection Identifiers for Advancing eco_restoration_shard Coder Tooling at Exabyte Scale

**Filename:** `coder_exabyte_scale_research_questions.md`  
**Destination:** `docs/research/coder_exabyte_scale_research_questions.md`

---

This document provides exactly 100 focused items—research questions, definition requests, detail queries, and objection identifiers—targeted at improving the **coder experience** (both human developers and AI coding agents) for the `eco_restoration_shard` project. The emphasis is on enabling the coder to work efficiently with **exabyte-scale storage** (via `/mnt/oss` VFS) and **hundreds of files generated at once** (via placeholder scaffolding and shard operations), while maintaining the ecosafety grammar, KER scoring, and Rust invariants.

Each item is labeled with its type:

- **RQ** – Research Question  
- **DR** – Definition Request  
- **DQ** – Detail Query  
- **OI** – Objection Identifier  

Use these items to guide further investigation, fill gaps in the current implementation, and harden the development workflow against scale-related failures.
